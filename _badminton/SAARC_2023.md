## Tournament Details
- Tournament: Minnesota SAARC Open 2023
- Event: Men's Doubles
- Location: Eden Prairie, MN
- Final Position: Champion (Men's Doubles A)

Today we won the Bangladeshi Badminton Tournament - 2023. It was a successful event with many awesome badminton enthusiasts performing. Thanks, [Syed Rashed Zaman](https://www.facebook.com/srzaman){:target="_blank"} for your trust in being my partner and pulling us to become unbeaten champions. Congratulations, Rusho. Thanks to [Mah Shahi](https://www.facebook.com/mahshahi){:target="_blank"} and [Fahim Murshed](https://www.facebook.com/murshed.fahim12){:target="_blank"} for putting on a good show in the final and congratulations on being runner-up. Congratulations to [Saleh Ahmed](https://www.facebook.com/sahmed2620){:target="_blank"} and Farrukh Hassan for being champion in the B bracket. Congratulations [Nazmul Islam Bipul](https://www.facebook.com/nazmul.islam.9638){:target="_blank"} bhai and Hafiz bhai for being runner-up in the B bracket. Congratulations [Imrose Alam](https://www.facebook.com/almd0201){:target="_blank"} and [Syed Abbas](https://www.facebook.com/syed.abbas.545849){:target="_blank"} for being champion in the C bracket.
A special thanks to the organizers [Furhath Arnob](https://www.facebook.com/furhath){:target="_blank"} and my friend [Wasim Akram](https://www.facebook.com/wasim.buet){:target="_blank"} for organizing this vibrant tournament for the community. The community wants to see more of this type of tournament. Thanks to all the participants and audience that made the tournament a colorful one. Heartfelt gratitude to [Salma Yeasmin](https://www.facebook.com/salmayeasmin.du){:target="_blank"} and Scion Sanjin for tolerating me talking about badminton tournament all the time for the last couple of weeks.  Thanks to [Bangladesh Athletic Club - Twin Cities](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1158535094609415){:target="_blank"} and its members for all the opportunities for practice, encouragement, and support. Last but not least, I feel gratitude to all my unofficial coaches - Ned Reaz, Vaskar Nalamothu, [Vignesh Ashokan](https://www.facebook.com/vignesh.asokan.9){:target="_blank"}, and Hafiz bhai.
