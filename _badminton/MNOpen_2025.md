## Tournament Details
- Tournament: Minnesota Open 2025
- Event: Mens's Singles, Men's Doubles, Mixed Doubles
- Location: Minneapolis, MN
- Final Position: Champion (Men's Doubles D), QF (Mixed Doubles C), QF (Men's Singles C)

It is that time of year again! The annual Labor Day weekend pilgrimage for Midwest badminton players—the [2025 MNBA Open](https://www.facebook.com/events/732048409429624/){:target="_blank"}! I'm thrilled to share that my partner, [Vignesh Asokan](https://www.facebook.com/vignesh.asokan.9){:target="_blank"}, and I not only survived but conquered, securing the championship title in Men's Doubles D. Thanks Vignesh, for sharing the court with me and trusting me. It was a truly heroic effort from both of us.

My journey in Men's Singles and Mixed Doubles with [Anusha Liyanage](https://www.facebook.com/anusha.liyanage.1){:target="_blank"} from Sri Lanka was also a wild ride. I started strong, but my championship dreams were unfortunately cut short in the C Quarter Finals for both events. Hey, you can't win 'em all, but securing Quarter Finals from the Round of 128 means something is loading in the near future. I will come back stronger!

A huge shout-out to my fellow [Bangladesh Athletic Club - Twin Cities](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1158535094609415){:target="_blank"} members. This year, [Syed Rashed Zaman](https://www.facebook.com/srzaman){:target="_blank"} and I are managing the BAC, and it was incredible to see so many people step out of their comfort zones and participate in the MNBA tournament. A special congratulations to our very own [Arzu Khan](https://www.facebook.com/arzu.khan.376){:target="_blank"} and [Karim Reza](https://www.facebook.com/MRK72){:target="_blank"} bhai for becoming runners-up in the Senior Men's Doubles. They showed up and conquered, a true "veni-vidi-vici" moment. Take a bow, brothers!!

Finally, the thank-you part! I couldn't have done this without the support of the entire BAC team. I would like to specially mention [Tanbeer Ahmed](https://www.facebook.com/tanbeer.ahmed.7){:target="_blank"}, [Shahadat Shimul](https://www.facebook.com/shahadathossain2017){:target="_blank"}, [Raffi Chowdury](https://www.facebook.com/raffi.chowdury.7){:target="_blank"}, [Saint Akhunji](https://www.facebook.com/saint.akhunji){:target="_blank"}, [Neamul Haque](https://www.facebook.com/neamulh){:target="_blank"}, [Wasim Akram](https://www.facebook.com/wasim.buet){:target="_blank"}, [Shahla Chowdhury](https://www.facebook.com/cs.diana){:target="_blank"}, [Sarah Khan](https://www.facebook.com/sarah.khan.295095){:target="_blank"}, [Furhath Arnob](https://www.facebook.com/furhath){:target="_blank"}, [Imrose Alam](https://www.facebook.com/almd0201){:target="_blank"}, [Syed Abbas](https://www.facebook.com/syed.abbas.545849){:target="_blank"}, [Syed Rashed Zaman](https://www.facebook.com/srzaman){:target="_blank"}, and [Bipul](https://www.facebook.com/nazmul.islam.9638){:target="_blank"} for encouraging me during the matches and for their phenomenal support. I would like to extend my gratitude to Ned Riaz and Bijoy for their advice as coaches.

I have gathered two trophies so far this year. I am dedicating one for each of my sons. And of course, a heartfelt thank you to my incredible wife for her inspiration and for managing the fort while I was out playing.

I am looking forward to other opportunities to play and conquer! Maybe I'll bring home enough trophies that I will need a new trophy cabinet like [Tottenham Hotspur](https://www.facebook.com/TottenhamHotspur){:target="_blank"}. Do I get any sponsorship for a trophy cabinet?
