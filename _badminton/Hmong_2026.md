## Tournament Details
- Tournament: Minnesota Hmong Open 2026
- Event: Men's Doubles, Mixed Doubles
- Location: Minneapolis, MN
- Final Position: Runner-up (Men's Doubles C), Champions (Mixed Doubles D)

Today marks the day for [Hmong MN Badminton Club Tournament](https://www.facebook.com/hmong.badminton.9){:target="_blank"}. This is the second most prestigious tournament after the Minnesota Open.
I am happy to share that I participated in two events and bagged two trophies. [Vignesh Asokan](https://www.facebook.com/vignesh.asokan.9){:target="_blank"} and I become runner up in Men's Double. [Anusha Liyanage](https://www.facebook.com/anusha.liyanage.16){:target="_blank"} and I claimed the Championship in the Mixed Double.

Thanks partners for making this a reality!

A huge shout-out to [Bangladesh Athletic Club - Twin Cities](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1158535094609415){:target="_blank"} for the opportunity to represent, practice, and support. I cannot express enough gratitude to all of you for coming to the venue and supporting me in this pursuit. Thank you, [Arzu Khan](https://www.facebook.com/arzu.khan.376){:target="_blank"}, [Shahriar Khan](https://www.facebook.com/shahriar.khan.35){:target="_blank"} bhai, [Syed Rashed Zaman](https://www.facebook.com/srzaman){:target="_blank"}. I am super lucky to have two youngsters chanting- go chacchu go! I won't be able make it without you cheering me up, Ryan and Wania!

A special thanks to my family especially my beautiful wifey for managing home while I have been playing the whole weekend. I dedicate two trophies to my two sons and prize money to [Suborna](https://www.facebook.com/salmayeasmin.du){:target="_blank"}. With this tournament, Suborna is a few dollar richer. Ha ha!

I am super charged and ready for the Interstate Badminton tournament organized by the [Bangladesh Sports Community of Chicagoland](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100090443921269){:target="_blank"} later this month.
