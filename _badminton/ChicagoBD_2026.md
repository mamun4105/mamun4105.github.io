## Tournament Details
- Tournament: Bangladesh Interstate Tournament 2026
- Event: Team Tournament
- Location: Naperville, IL
- Final Position: Semi-Finalist (Men's Doubles)

I had the honor of representing the [Bangladesh Athletic Club - Twin Cities](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1158535094609415){:target="_blank"} at the Bangladesh Interstate Tournament 2026! It was a fantastic event. I really enjoyed the top class Bangladeshi badminton players from different parts of the US. [Syed Rashed Zaman](https://www.facebook.com/srzaman){:target="_blank"} and I played Men's Doubles and reached the semi-finals.
