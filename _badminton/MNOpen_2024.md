## Tournament Details
- Tournament: Minnesota Open 2025
- Event: Mens's Singles, Men's Doubles
- Location: Minneapolis, MN
- Final Position: Champion (Men's Singles D), Third Round (Men's Doubles)

I am extremely happy for participating in the 2024 MN Olympic edition badminton tournament. It was an exciting experience to play with and watch all these badminton enthusiasts from around the country. I participated in two events - Men's Single and Men's Double. While Men's Single is not in my wheelhouse, I become the champion in the D bracket. As this year we had the Olympic edition, I was part of an independent group comprising Bangladesh, Pakistan and people who prefer not to associate themselves to any country. In this country group, I got a gold medal for Bangladesh.

[Syed Rashed Zaman](https://www.facebook.com/srzaman){:target="_blank"} and I played in the Men's double. We lost at the third round. Rusho, we will come back stronger.

Thanks to Minnesota Badminton for arranging this event. Thanks to our [Bangladesh Athletic Club - Twin Cities](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1158535094609415){:target="_blank"} for all the opportunities to practice and support. A special thanks to [Salma Yeasmin](https://www.facebook.com/salmayeasmin.du){:target="_blank"} and Scion Sanjin for their encouragement.
