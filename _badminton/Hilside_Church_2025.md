## Tournament Details
- Tournament: Minnesota Hmong Open 2026
- Event: Team Tournament
- Location: Minneapolis, MN
- Final Position: Champions (Team Tournament)

I was thrilled to participate in a fun, local tournament here in Minnesota, organized by the [Hillside Church Badminton Ministry](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1158535094609415){:target="_blank"}. This year's theme was anime and cartoons, which made for a really creative and enjoyable event. I had the honor of leading Team Airbenders, and I'm proud to say we bent the competition to our will! We had an incredible team whose "airbending" skills were truly top-notch. We ultimately dominated the Mighty Simbas in the finals to take home the championship!

I'd like to extend a huge thank you to all the organizers for putting together such a wonderful event. I also want to express my sincere gratitude to my better half for her unwavering support, especially with a newborn at home. Finally, a special thank you to my sons- Scion and Sanid, whose inspiration fueled me throughout every match.

With that, I'm already looking forward to the [2025 MNBA Open](https://www.facebook.com/events/732048409429624/){:target="_blank"} at the end of August. Wish me luck!
