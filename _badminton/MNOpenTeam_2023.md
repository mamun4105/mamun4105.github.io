## Tournament Details
- Tournament: Minnesota Team Tournamenr 2023
- Event: Team Tournament
- Location: Burnsville, MN
- Final Position: Champion A

After moving to the Twin Cities, I have resumed badminton after decades. So far I have participated in three MN open tournaments including today's team tournament. In the last two tournaments, I was not successful, but learned a lot, tremendously enjoyed, and proud to play alongside awesome players. Meanwhile, I kept practicing almost 4 days a week and won several club level tournaments. But I had high hopes of bagging something from the MN open. Friends and family, today is the day! The day I will remember many moons to come. Our team won the 2023 MN winter badminton team tournament. We become the champion in A bracket. It was a super fun event and I enjoyed every bit of it. I feel so proud of playing with and against some of the badminton maestros.

Thanks to [Bangladesh Athletic Club - Twin Cities](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1158535094609415){:target="_blank"} for the opportunity to practice and support. Congratulations [Syed Rashed Zaman](https://www.facebook.com/srzaman){:target="_blank"} from BAC to become runner up in the B bracket. Special gratitude to [Salma Yeasmin](https://www.facebook.com/salma.yeasmin.125){:target="_blank"} and Scion Sanjin for their support and encouragement.
